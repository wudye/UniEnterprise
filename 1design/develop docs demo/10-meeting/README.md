## Meeting Types

### 1. Requirements Review Meeting

**Purpose**: Review and approve requirements

**Participants**: PM, BA, Architects, Developers

**Agenda**:
- Review requirements
- Identify gaps and issues
- Assign action items
- Approve requirements

**Frequency**: As needed

### 2. Architecture Review Meeting

**Purpose**: Review and approve architecture design

**Participants**: Architects, Senior Developers, PM

**Agenda**:
- Review architecture design
- Discuss technical concerns
- Make architecture decisions
- Approve architecture

**Frequency**: As needed

### 3. Sprint Planning Meeting

**Purpose**: Plan sprint work items

**Participants**: PM, Developers, Testers

**Agenda**:
- Review backlog
- Select sprint items
- Estimate effort
- Assign tasks
- Define sprint goal

**Frequency**: Every sprint (2-4 weeks)

### 4. Daily Standup Meeting

**Purpose**: Sync team progress and issues

**Participants**: All team members

**Agenda** (15 minutes max):
- What did I complete yesterday?
- What will I do today?
- What are my blockers?

**Frequency**: Daily

### 5. Sprint Review Meeting

**Purpose**: Review completed work

**Participants**: PM, Developers, Testers, Stakeholders

**Agenda**:
- Demo completed work
- Collect feedback
- Review sprint goals
- Discuss completed items

**Frequency**: Every sprint

### 6. Sprint Retrospective Meeting

**Purpose**: Reflect on sprint and identify improvements

**Participants**: All team members

**Agenda**:
- What went well?
- What didn't go well?
- What can we improve?
- Create action items

**Frequency**: Every sprint

### 7. Technical Review Meeting

**Purpose**: Review technical designs and solutions

**Participants**: Architects, Senior Developers, Developers

**Agenda**:
- Review technical design
- Discuss implementation approach
- Identify technical risks
- Make technical decisions

**Frequency**: As needed

## Meeting Best Practices

### 1. Before the Meeting

- **Prepare agenda**: Send agenda at least 24 hours in advance
- **Review materials**: Review relevant documents before meeting
- **Invite right people**: Only invite necessary participants

### 2. During the Meeting

- **Start on time**: Respect everyone's time
- **Follow agenda**: Stick to the agenda items
- **Take notes**: Assign note taker
- **Capture decisions**: Document all decisions
- **Assign action items**: Clear owner and due date

### 3. After the Meeting

- **Send minutes**: Send meeting minutes within 24 hours
- **Follow up**: Follow up on action items
- **Store records**: Store meeting records in shared location
