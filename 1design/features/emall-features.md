# E-commerce Mall Features

| Category | Features | Description |
|:----------|:---------|:------------|
| **Product Center** | Product SPU/SKU, Product Categories, Product Reviews, Product Attributes, Product Recommendations, Product Brands | Central management system for all product-related data including standardized product information (SPU/SKU), categorization, customer feedback, attributes, brand management, and recommendation algorithms |
| **Transaction Center** | Order Management, After-sales Refund, Express Delivery, Store Pickup, Shopping Cart, Sales Commission | Comprehensive transaction handling system covering the entire order lifecycle from creation to delivery, including refund processing, multiple delivery methods, cart functionality, and affiliate commission management |
| **Marketing Center** | Coupon Codes, Flash Sales, Bargain Activities, Limited-time Discounts, Full Reduction Promotions | Powerful marketing toolkit with various promotional strategies including coupon distribution, time-limited flash sales, social bargaining campaigns, discount promotions, and conditional sales incentives |
| **Member Center** | Member Management, Member Levels, Member Tags, Member Points, Points Check-in, Member Groups, Shipping Addresses | Complete customer relationship management system for user engagement featuring tiered membership, loyalty points system, gamification through check-ins, user segmentation, and address book management |

---

## Detailed Feature Explanations

### Product Center
- **Product SPU/SKU**: Standardized product information management (SPU = Standard Product Unit, SKU = Stock Keeping Unit)
- **Product Categories**: Organize products into hierarchical category structures for easy browsing and management
- **Product Reviews**: Collect and display customer reviews and ratings to build trust and improve products
- **Product Attributes**: Manage product specifications, variants, and custom attributes (size, color, material, etc.)
- **Product Recommendations**: Algorithm-based product suggestions to increase cross-selling and up-selling opportunities
- **Product Brands**: Manage brand information and establish brand pages for enhanced brand identity

### Transaction Center
- **Order Management**: Complete order lifecycle management from creation to completion and archiving
- **After-sales Refund**: Handle refund and return requests with automated or manual approval workflows
- **Express Delivery**: Integration with logistics providers for real-time tracking and delivery management
- **Store Pickup**: Support click-and-collect functionality allowing customers to pick up orders at physical stores
- **Shopping Cart**: Persistent shopping cart functionality with quantity management and price calculations
- **Sales Commission**: Automated commission tracking and calculation for affiliates and sales representatives

### Marketing Center
- **Coupon Codes**: Create and distribute promotional codes with various discount types and usage conditions
- **Flash Sales**: Time-limited promotional events with countdown timers and limited inventory
- **Bargain Activities**: Social sharing campaigns where users invite friends to reduce product prices
- **Limited-time Discounts**: Scheduled price reductions for specific products during promotional periods
- **Full Reduction Promotions**: Conditional discounts triggered when cart total reaches specified thresholds

### Member Center
- **Member Management**: Comprehensive user account management with profile and preference settings
- **Member Levels**: Tiered membership system with exclusive benefits based on spending or activity
- **Member Tags**: Flexible user segmentation for targeted marketing and personalized experiences
- **Member Points**: Loyalty reward system for earning and redeeming points through purchases and activities
- **Points Check-in**: Daily check-in feature allowing users to earn points and maintain engagement
- **Member Groups**: User group management for bulk operations and targeted campaigns
- **Shipping Addresses**: Address book management for quick checkout and multiple delivery locations
